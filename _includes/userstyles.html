AIOS Sidebar Autohide
The AIOS sidebar appears when far left of screen is clicked. It hides when the mouse is moved away from the AIOS sidebar. Requires: All-in-One Sidebar https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/all-in-one-sidebar/?src=userprofile [screenshot]
Bookmarks Toolbar Autohide upon Hover
The bookmarks toolbar appears when mouse hovers on awesome bar. It hides when mouse is moved away. [screenshot]
